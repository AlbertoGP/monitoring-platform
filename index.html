View the latest information on oil spills in Nigeria

Data from the National Oil Spill Detection and Response Agency (NOSDRA) database.

NOSDRA is working hard to improve the integrity of this data, but relies heavily on the oil companies to supply information and update this regularly.

NOSDRA hopes this new initiative will improve the monitoring and tracking of oil spills in Nigeria, acknowledges that this data may be incomplete or inaccurate, and is working to improve this situation.
